Problem Statement

The poor quality of many Tunisian highways, which are often narrow and bumpy due to substandard materials and improper construction, leads to severe consequences. These include frequent traffic congestion, an increased risk of accidents, delays for emergency services, and significant vehicle damage, ultimately resulting in high repair costs for drivers and substantial economic losses for the country.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To innovate and build safer, more efficient, and sustainable roads that last, ensuring safer travel and supporting **SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure**.

Our Vision: A future where Tunisia's infrastructure is a model of economic growth and safety, free from congestion and risk, connecting communities and driving prosperity.
